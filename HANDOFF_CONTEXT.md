## Important Caveats

Not all source quality is equal.

Downweight:

- Reddit threads
- generic SEO blogs
- design showcase sites
- weak market-number pages without clear provenance

Prefer:

- official product and support pages
- high-quality market reports
- app store reviews
- trusted company / funding databases
- mobile app intelligence tools
