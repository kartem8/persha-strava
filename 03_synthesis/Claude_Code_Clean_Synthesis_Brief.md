## Important Evidence Quality Notes

Not all cited sources in the collected outputs are equally strong.

High-trust sources:

- official Strava product, support, pricing, and press pages
- official competitor product and support pages
- WHO, CDC, OECD, Global Wellness Institute
- Microsoft, OpenAI, Google, Anthropic official product pages when relevant

Medium-trust sources:

- Business of Apps
- Sensor Tower style industry summaries
- reputable trade press and market overviews

Low-trust or downweight:

- Reddit threads
- Alibaba content pages
- design showcase sites
- generic blogs comparing running apps
- weak or unclear SEO pages
- unsupported claims about exact user counts, retention lifts, or internal unit economics

Instruction:

If there is a conflict between a weak source and an official or higher-trust source, prefer the higher-trust source.
