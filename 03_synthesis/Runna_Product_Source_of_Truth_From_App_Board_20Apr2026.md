# Runna Product Source of Truth From App Board

## Purpose

Цей документ фіксує, що саме `Runna` already собою представляє на основі борду
`01_inputs/Runna App.png`.

Його задача:

- мати окремий product baseline по `Runna`
- трактувати `Runna` як частину ширшої Strava surface area для overlap-аналізу
- не переплутати "це є в Strava core app" і "це вже є в Strava-owned Runna"
- зрозуміти, які expansion-ідеї вже частково або сильно перекриваються через Runna

## Executive Summary

На цьому борді `Runna` виглядає не просто як "app with running plans".

Runna already looks like a broader running platform that combines:

- structured running plans and workout execution
- account / preferences / device integrations
- shoes and gear-adjacent tracking
- support content on training, injury, nutrition, strength and triathlon
- race discovery and community events
- in-app community discussions
- loyalty / levels / offers / partner discounts
- merch and media extensions like store and podcast

Ключовий висновок:

**Якщо ми дивимося на expansion ideas для Strava, треба враховувати не лише Strava core app, а й те, що Runna already закриває цілий coaching-and-running lifestyle layer.**

Це особливо важливо для ідей навколо:

- coaching
- recovery / return-to-activity
- race & event marketplace
- nutrition
- creator / community support

## What This Artifact Suggests About Runna's Product Model

На основі цього борду Runna already spans these product jobs:

1. `Tell me what to do`
Структуровані running workouts and plan logic.

2. `Help me execute it`
Device sync, recording, wearables, heart-rate zones, calendars.

3. `Help me train smarter`
Training hub, guidance content, preferences, coaching-like support.

4. `Help me stay healthy enough to keep training`
Injury management, strength & mobility, stretch-plan references, nutrition guidance.

5. `Help me progress toward race day`
Race workout type, find-a-race surfaces, events, community events.

6. `Keep me engaged between workouts`
Levels, rewards, offers, podcast, community discussions.

## What Runna Does Not Clearly Prove

Not proven by this artifact:

- human coach marketplace
- direct 1:1 coach-to-athlete workspace
- live coach messaging as a core surface
- meal logging / macro tracking
- medical-grade recovery scoring
- booking engine for travel / experiences
- organizer-grade event tooling
- full commerce marketplace beyond merch and partner offers

## Strategic Implications For Strava Expansion Ideas

Нижче не verdict про те, що ідея мертва.
Це про те, що `Runna`, як частина Strava surface area, already reduces the whitespace.

## Highest overlap

### 1. Coach Marketplace

Overlap level:

- Medium-High

Why:

- Runna already deeply owns structured training, workout sequencing, connected execution, and guidance content.
- На борді не видно human marketplace, але app-based coaching value proposition already strong.

Remaining whitespace:

- coach discovery
- coach matching
- coach purchase flow
- coach-side CRM / workspace
- direct coach-athlete collaboration layer

### 2. Strava Ready / Recovery & Longevity

Overlap level:

- Medium-High

Why:

- Runna already has injury management, strength & mobility, stretch-plan references, recovery-adjacent offers and guidance.

Remaining whitespace:

- readiness score
- HRV / sleep / wearable-led recovery intelligence
- deeper longitudinal recovery model

### 3. Return-to-Activity / Pre-Hab

Overlap level:

- High

Why:

- Injury management and return-support content already appear directly in the help and support surfaces.

Remaining whitespace:

- safer structured progression protocols
- stronger clinical / physio layer
- post-injury personalization

### 4. Strava Live / Race & Event Marketplace

Overlap level:

- Medium-High

Why:

- Runna already includes find-a-race, events, upcoming events, and race-adjacent community surfaces.

Remaining whitespace:

- registration commerce
- organizer monetization
- full race booking and operations infra

## Lowest overlap

## Bottom Line

Якщо дивитися на `Runna` як на частину Strava ecosystem, то Strava already has a much stronger position in:

- structured coaching
- running guidance
- race-adjacent planning
- injury and training support
- runner community
- partner offers and loyalty

ніж це видно з core Strava app alone.

Тому при shortlist evaluation важливо не питати лише:

- `Чи є це у Strava main app?`

а ще й:

- `Чи це already partially owned by Runna, which Strava now controls?`

Найбільший practical impact це має для ідей:

- `Coach Marketplace`
- `Recovery / Pre-Hab`
- `Race & Event Marketplace`
- `Nutrition`

Бо ці зони вже частково occupied not by Strava UI alone, but by the combined Strava + Runna product surface.
