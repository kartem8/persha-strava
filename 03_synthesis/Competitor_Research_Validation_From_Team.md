## What should be treated carefully

Several claims in the document are directionally interesting but too weak or too specific to use as-is in final materials without stronger sourcing.

Examples:

- exact user-segment splits like `50% users = 80% revenue`
- exact audience-share claims by segment without primary source support
- exact category counts / challenge counts without a strong source trail
- exact monetization figures like campaign prices or contract sizes unless verified independently

These are okay as internal exploration signals, but should not be treated as final presentation facts yet.
